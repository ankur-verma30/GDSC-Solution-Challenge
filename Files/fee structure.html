INTRODUCTION

Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya came into an establishment to create academic excellence. The university offers courses in Arts, Commerce, Education, Engineering and Technology, Humanities, Life Sciences, Management, Pharmacy, Sciences, and Social Sciences. The GGU Bilaspur fee structure details for major courses are listed below: The fee structure of GGU Bilaspur B.Tech program is INR 28,850 for 1st year, INR 27,050 for the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th year. GGU Bilaspur MBA fees is INR 25,850 for 1st year and INR 24,050 for 2nd year. GGU Bilaspur fee structure for the B.Ed program is INR 11,850 for 1st year and INR 10,050 for 2nd year.


The GGU is a renowned university that aims to impart quality education to students with the help of advanced teaching standards. The university follows a systematic pattern that includes a blend of theory classes with practical sessions. Some of the popular course offered are: Bachelor of Science [B.Sc] Bachelors of Technology [B.Tech] Master of Business Administration [MBA] You can choose from over 18 UG, 33 PG, 1 Diploma, 15 Dual Degree, and 26 Doctorate courses at GGU



Entrance Exams Accepted by GGU Bilaspur

Entries to the university are conducted based on marks obtained in Vishwavidyalaya Entrance Test [VET]. Other exams accepted are:

  1. JEE Main
  2. GPAT
  3. CMAT
  4. CSIR NET
  5. UGC NET
  6. VRET [Vishwavidyalaya Research Entrance Test]


GGU Bilaspur Fee Payment Guidelines

Candidates who have enrolled can pay the fees online. The options allowed for online payment at the university are:

  1. Pay through Net Banking/ Debit/ Credit Card/ UPI
  2. Pay through Paytm.com
